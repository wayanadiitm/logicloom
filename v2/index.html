F.A.Q

  • What's the eligibility to participate?

    This is a PAN BS event. All students (irrespective of the level) enrolled (i.e. having the student mail id) in the IITM UG Data Science or Electronic Systems programme are welcome to register for our event.

  • I'm a foundational student. Should I take part in this contest?

    Definitely. We highly encourage you to register. Although this is a 'beta' event, we have separated FL students into a different category for the prelims. The difficulty level for this round will be set considering it as a 'gamma' event.
    Also the cut-off percentile for prelims will be different for FL students, taking into consideration, the difficulty factor and the performance statistics.
    Pre-requisites: For the first round, foundation level knowledge, with basic programming skills should be fine. Proficiency in critical thinking, problem solving and ability to approach challenges creatively and analytically is the key! Having familiarity with fundamental concepts in AI and skills in technical domain will be a plus.

  • I am unable to access the registration form. What to do?

    Please try signing into the Paradox portal using the IITM smail id only. This should resolve the issue.
    If you are yet to receive the student mail, please communicate with the Paradox team directly at support@iitmparadox.org.

  • I can't login to the LogicLooM portal. It shows incorrect credentials.

    This happens only when atleast one of the credentials is incorrectly entered. Please cross-check your smail address and password. The details would have been sent to your registered smail via an auto-mail days back. Check your spam folder as well incase it's not available in the inbox and shift it to 'Primary' if that's the case.
    Please write to logicloom@ds.study.iitm.ac.in for any queries.

  • Due to some issues in my internet connectivity I couldn't submit the prelims. Will it be fine?

    Absolutely, there's nothing to worry. The responses marked before the glitch occured, will be saved at the backend and evaluated by the auto-grader once the due time passes for your profile. It will be considered as a submission, provided all the integrity checks pass once processed by the system (Once the exam ends, all activity logs from the portal will be processed via our system. The entire process is automated. Any cases of discrepancies, mismatch, UFM will be tracked by our tool and will result in the failed checks, followed by the cancellation of your exam attempt and event participation)
    Also, for any loading issues on the question screen (due to server load etc.), press the refresh icon on the exam screen.

  • I logged in to the LogicLooM portal at 7PM. Is that my start time?

    No. Once you login, you will land on the waiting lounge where you can view the quiz related instructions. There should be a 'Launch' button which will get auto-activated after the prelims window gets live.
    Clicking this button will only start the quiz and take you to the main questions page, which will be considered as your start time and you need to submit within 30 mins from then. Please refer to the live timer on the portal.

  • I started the prelims at 23:45PM. Will I still get 30 minutes?

    No. You will have to finish on or before 23:59 IST (after which the attempt window will close). To utilise the complete duration, you need to start the assessment latest by 23.30 IST. Otherwise, you’ll get less time to answer.
    Please go through the rule book properly.

  • Can I take prelims in both the attempt windows?

    No. Once you start the exam, you have to complete it within the slot in the specified time limit. The maximum number of attempts will be set to 1.
    Also, each category (Foundational and Diploma-Degree) has its designated slot. Please check the 'Important Dates' section on the website.

  • I have some other questions that haven't been discussed here.

    We suggest you to read the event Rule book (logicloom.netlify.app/rules) first, as everything has been explained there in detail.
    Still, if you have any general questions or face technical issues, feel free to contact the organizers via mail at logicloom@ds.study.iitm.ac.in. We will try to reply back within one working day.
    You may also contact our Public relations member at +91 7737861998.
